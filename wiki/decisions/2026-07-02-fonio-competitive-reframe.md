# Competitive reframe: fonio.ai kills the "nobody does German + GDPR" thesis

## Decision

Competitive research on 2026-07-02 (full record:
`wiki/competitors/2026-07-02-fonio-ai.md`) established that fonio.ai —
Vienna, $17M seed at $140M valuation (Jun 2026, led by 20VC), self-reported
7,000+ customers — is German-first, GDPR-native (proprietary EU stack,
Nuremberg hosting), and already ships WhatsApp as a €79/mo add-on on top of
€99–499/mo voice plans.

`PROJECT.md` §4 was updated (same date) to reflect this. The operative
changes to how Tilda is positioned and sold:

1. **The gap thesis narrows.** "WhatsApp-first + German + GDPR + local is a
   gap the US players don't cover" is no longer true as stated — fonio covers
   German + GDPR + local. The surviving, defensible gap is **WhatsApp-first +
   beauty-niche depth + done-for-you local service**. fonio is voice-first
   and horizontal; WhatsApp is their bolt-on, not their wedge.
2. **Never sell the generic "AI receptionist" category in DACH** — that
   category is fonio's to win with a €178/mo self-serve anchor (Solo +
   WhatsApp add-on) and no setup fee. Sell **missed-booking recovery** as a
   done-for-you service; the €250–400/mo + €500–1,500 setup price only holds
   against service value, not software value.
3. **Don't fight on infrastructure sovereignty.** fonio's proprietary EU
   stack beats our Twilio/ElevenLabs/OpenRouter assembly on paper.
   Neutralize compliance (DPAs, EU-residency modes, per-client sub-accounts)
   and move the conversation to outcomes (recovered bookings, ROI proof).

## Rationale

The original §4 framing ("almost nobody is WhatsApp-first, US players don't
cover Germany") predated fonio's visibility and Jun 2026 funding. Selling
against a stale competitive map risks pitching a "gap" a well-funded local
incumbent already fills — worst case, in front of a prospect who already got
fonio's marketing.

## Watch items / reconsideration triggers

Re-run the intel (quarterly, or on news): fonio email/web-chat launch
(~Sept 2026), AI-native CRM (end Q3 2026), WhatsApp promoted from add-on to
core, German pricing moves, any beauty-vertical marketing. If fonio goes
WhatsApp-first for beauty at self-serve pricing, the remaining wedge is
service depth + business memory only — revisit positioning then.
